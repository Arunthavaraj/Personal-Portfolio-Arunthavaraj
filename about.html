Who Am I?

A research-oriented professional with a keen fascination for the cosmos and a strong passion for data exploration and machine learning models. I bring over two years of experience as an Analyst in the SAP domain at Accenture, coupled with a Master’s degree in Data Science and Analytics from the University of Leeds. My expertise spans various data analytics tools, complemented by hands-on experience across multiple impactful projects.

Why Work With Me

I have always seen data as a puzzle complex, yet rewarding once it all fits together. With an MSc, I have built a strong foundation in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data modelling. From predicting customer behaviour to analysing rainfall patterns, I focus on using data to drive meaningful insights. For my dissertation, completed in collaboration with the Met Office, I applied unsupervised learning techniques to classify rainfall patterns, demonstrating my ability to solve complex data problems. At Accenture, I worked as an Analyst in the field of SAP, collaborating closely with clients to deliver over 10 successful SAP system upgrades. I ensured zero downtime and maintained 100% SLA uptime by managing multiple teams and streamlining processes. My role involved gathering business requirements, cleaning data, and deploying solutions that were implemented smoothly into production. I have strong technical skills in Python, R, SQL, and advanced Excel, which I use to extract valuable insights from data and support decision-making.

I’ve also contributed to various projects such as predicting the success of ICO's and analysing customer churn, applying data-driven approaches to improve business outcomes. My internships at Smazee Software Solutions and Kairos Exe Technologies allowed me to gain hands-on experience in app development and application testing, which broadened my technical expertise.

Additionally, I worked as a Coding Tutor at Hatch School of Code, teaching Python, R, and Data Science to students under 15, which enhanced my ability to explain complex concepts in a simple, understandable way. I bring a combination of technical skills, practical experience, and a problem-solving mindset that makes me an asset to any business. My ability to analyse data, deliver actionable insights, and collaborate effectively with teams enables me to drive informed decision-making and contribute to the success of the organisation.
